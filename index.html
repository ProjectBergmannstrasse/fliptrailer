SEC 01 / MECHANISM

How the machine works

No RSI, no moving averages. Five moves, repeated forever. All dollar examples use 0.01 lots where 1 point = $0.01 — multiply by 100 for 1.00 lot.

1

Cold start — the straddle

With no trade open, the EA brackets the market: a BUY STOP 60 pts above price and a SELL STOP 60 pts below. First one hit becomes the live trade; the other is cancelled.

gold @ $3,300.00 → BUY STOP $3,300.60 · SELL STOP $3,299.40
price spikes up → BUY opens @ $3,300.60 · sell stop cancelled
2

The flip line is armed

The instant a trade opens, an opposite pending stop appears on the other side. That order is the stop loss — same price level. One line does both jobs.

long from $3,300.60 → SELL STOP @ $3,300.00
if price falls there: buy closes −$0.60, sell opens — same tick
3

Breakeven lock — the key protection

At +30 pts of profit (+$0.30), the flip line snaps past entry. From that moment the leg mathematically cannot lose. This is where the 80% win rate comes from.

price hits $3,300.90 (+$0.30) → flip line jumps $3,300.00 → $3,300.70
worst case is now +$0.10 guaranteed
4

The trail

After the lock, the line follows the last closed M1 candle's extreme with a 15-pt buffer. It only tightens — never retreats. Winners run as long as the trend holds.

price runs to $3,302.00 · last candle low $3,301.50
flip line = 3,301.50 − 0.15 = $3,301.35 → locking +$0.75
5

The flip

Price reverses through the line: old leg books its locked profit, opposite leg opens at the same level, a fresh flip line is armed 60 pts behind. Back to step 2.

buy closes +$0.75 @ $3,301.35 → sell opens @ $3,301.35 → BUY STOP armed @ $3,301.95

SEC 04 / THE REAL COST

The spread problem

The backtests charged zero spread. Reality doesn't. At 66,018 trades, every single point of average spread costs $660 across the test (0.01 lots). Drag the slider — this is the whole game.

Breakeven is 69.8 pts. If your broker's average spread across all trades is above ~70 points, this strategy loses money — full stop. Check yours: MT5 → Settings → yellow $ button → Custom spread → re-run at 30 / 50 / 70.

Cutting the cost: use a raw/commission account (spread 15–25 pts), enable the session filter (10:00–21:00 server — London + NY, where the profit actually lives), and set max spread = 35 so the EA refuses to arm into news blowouts.
